Our Story

About Accucare Nurse Staffing in Houston, TX

Since 2004, Accucare has been the Greater Houston area's trusted partner for compassionate and dependable healthcare staffing solutions, serving patients, families, and providers across every care setting.

Founded 2004

Twenty Years of Serving the Greater Houston Area

Accucare Nurse Staffing was founded in 2004 with a straightforward goal: give patients, families, and healthcare providers across the Greater Houston area access to qualified, compassionate nursing professionals they could actually count on.

For two decades, our reputation has come from doing the work right. When a hospice agency calls at 11 PM needing a continuous care nurse, we answer. When a nursing home director has a last-minute no-call-no-show on a holiday weekend, Accucare comes through. That consistency, shift after shift and year after year, is what has made us a trusted partner to the Greater Houston area healthcare community.

Our founder understood early on that the relationship between a staffing agency and the patients, families, and providers it serves is built on one thing above all else: trust. Trust that the clinician showing up will be qualified. Trust that the agency will be responsive. Trust that standards won't slip under pressure.

Those principles guide everything we do today: how we recruit and vet our clinicians, how we respond to after-hours requests, and how we handle every placement with the same care and diligence as the very first one.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Three things guide how we operate.

Compassion

Healthcare is personal. Every clinician we place brings both clinical skill and genuine care for the patients, families, and teams they work with. We look for people who understand that showing up and doing the job well is only part of it.

Dependability

Families and providers count on us to show up. Every time. We're available 24/7, we respond quickly, and when we confirm a shift, that shift is covered. That's something we take seriously.

Clinical Excellence

Every nurse and caregiver in our network undergoes rigorous credential verification, background screening, and skills assessment. We do not compromise on clinical standards. Your patients' safety depends on it.
