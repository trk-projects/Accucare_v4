Our Story

About Accucare Nurse Staffing in Houston, TX

Since 2004, Accucare has been the Greater Houston area's trusted partner for compassionate and dependable healthcare staffing solutions, serving patients, families, and providers across every care setting.

Founded 2004 · Family-Owned

Built From the Ground Up. Not a Franchise.

Accucare Nurse Staffing was founded in 2004 — not as a corporate franchise or an investor-backed operation, but as a Houston business built from the ground up by someone who understood healthcare from the inside. That origin is still part of how we operate today.

When you call Accucare, you're not routed through a call center. You're not a ticket in a system. You talk to the owner. Decisions get made fast, accountability is direct, and the person responsible for your staffing relationship is the same person who built this agency — shift by shift, year by year.

For over two decades, that ownership mentality has been our edge. When a hospice agency calls at 11 PM needing a continuous care nurse, we answer. When a nursing home director has a last-minute no-call-no-show on a holiday weekend, Accucare comes through. That consistency is what has made us a trusted partner across the Greater Houston healthcare community.

Franchise agencies operate on volume. Accucare operates on relationships. The difference shows in every placement we make.

Why We Exist

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission
"Compassionate and dependable healthcare staffing — for every provider, every setting, every shift."

Accucare exists to make skilled, trustworthy nursing coverage available to every healthcare facility that needs it — especially in the moments when the stakes are highest and other agencies aren't answering the phone.

Our Vision
"To be the staffing partner that Houston’s healthcare community calls first — and counts on every time."

We're building toward a Greater Houston where no hospice patient goes without care, no facility is left scrambling for coverage, and every nurse we place knows they're part of a team that has their back.

Our Approach

Concierge-Level Staffing. Owner Accountability.

Most staffing agencies treat placements like transactions. We treat them like partnerships — and the person you speak to is the same person who stands behind every shift we fill.

A Real Person. Every Call.

When you call Accucare, a living, breathing human being answers — not a bot, not a phone tree, not a voicemail system. You speak directly with someone who knows the business and can act immediately. No hold music during a staffing crisis. No automated ticket systems. Just a real person who picks up and helps.

Built on Accountability

Every clinician we place carries our name. We vet thoroughly, we stand behind our placements, and if something isn't right, we fix it — personally. That's what it means to be independently owned and operated.

Long-Term Relationships

We know our providers. We know our nurses. Over 20 years of operating in Houston, we've built real relationships — not just a roster. When we match a clinician to your facility, it's a considered placement, not a slot-fill.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Three things guide how we operate.

Compassion

Healthcare is personal. Every clinician we place brings both clinical skill and genuine care for the patients, families, and teams they work with. We look for people who understand that showing up and doing the job well is only part of it.

Dependability

Families and providers count on us to show up. Every time. We're available 24/7, we respond quickly, and when we confirm a shift, that shift is covered. That's something we take seriously.

Clinical Excellence

Every nurse and caregiver in our network undergoes rigorous credential verification, background screening, and skills assessment. We do not compromise on clinical standards. Your patients' safety depends on it.
