Our Story

About Accucare Nurse Staffing in Houston, TX

Since 2004, Accucare has provided dependable healthcare staffing to patients, families, and providers across the Greater Houston area, covering every care setting from hospice to long-term care.

Founded 2004 · Family Founded

Family Founded, Two Decades in the Making

Accucare Nurse Staffing has been part of Houston's healthcare community since 2004. It didn't start as a franchise or a corporate rollout. It was family founded, built by people who believed healthcare staffing should feel personal and not like something processed by a system somewhere.

In those early years it was a small operation built on relationships and hard work. Calls were answered directly. Every facility that trusted Accucare with a shift mattered. Over time, that approach earned more providers, more nurses, and more referrals. Word spread through doing the work right and showing up consistently.

More than 20 years later, Accucare is still that company. We've grown steadily and kept things personal along the way. We're a Houston company, not a national staffing chain managing this city as a line item on a spreadsheet. Call us at any hour and a real person answers. Someone who knows the work, understands the urgency, and is ready to help.

Our Approach

Personal Staffing. Real Accountability.

At most staffing agencies, placements go through a system. Accucare has always run on relationships. That's how this business was built, and that's how it still runs today.

A Real Person. Every Call.

When you call Accucare, a real person picks up. No bots, no phone trees, no voicemail. Someone who knows the work, understands the urgency, and can act on your request right away.

Built on Accountability

Every clinician we place carries our name. We vet thoroughly, stand behind our placements, and if something isn't right, we fix it personally. That's what being independently owned and operated means.

Long-Term Relationships

We know our providers. We know our nurses. Over 20 years in Houston, we've built genuine relationships with the people and facilities we work with. When we match a clinician to your facility, it's a considered placement made with your specific setting in mind.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Three things guide how we operate.

Compassion

Healthcare is personal. Every clinician we place brings both clinical skill and genuine care for the patients, families, and teams they work with. We look for people who understand that showing up and doing the job well is only part of it.

Dependability

Families and providers count on us to show up. Every time. We're available 24/7, we respond quickly, and when we confirm a shift, that shift is covered. That's something we take seriously.

Clinical Excellence

Every nurse and caregiver in our network undergoes rigorous credential verification, background screening, and skills assessment. We do not compromise on clinical standards. Your patients' safety depends on it.
