Founded 2004

Twenty Years of Serving Houston's Healthcare Community

Accucare Nurse Staffing was founded in 2004 with a clear and enduring mission: to provide patients, families, and healthcare providers across the Houston metropolitan area with the most reliable, compassionate, and clinically qualified nursing professionals available.

For two decades, we've built our reputation not through advertising, but through performance. When a hospice agency calls at 11 PM needing a continuous care nurse, we answer. When a nursing home director has a last-minute no-call-no-show on a holiday weekend, Accucare comes through. That consistency, shift after shift and year after year, is what has made us a trusted partner to Houston's healthcare community.

Our founder understood early on that the relationship between a staffing agency and the patients, families, and providers it serves is built on one thing above all else: trust. Trust that the clinician showing up will be qualified. Trust that the agency will be responsive. Trust that standards won't slip under pressure.

Those principles guide everything we do today: how we recruit and vet our clinicians, how we respond to after-hours requests, and how we handle every placement with the same care and diligence as the very first one.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Three principles shape every decision we make, every clinician we place, and every relationship we build.

Compassion

Healthcare is a deeply human endeavor. Every clinician we place brings not just clinical skill but genuine empathy toward patients, families, and the teams they support. We hire and retain professionals who understand that care means more than completing a task.

Dependability

Families and providers count on us to show up. Every time. We've built our model around 24/7 availability, rapid response, and a firm commitment to our word. If we confirm a shift, that shift is covered. That reliability is non-negotiable at Accucare.

Clinical Excellence

Every nurse and caregiver in our network undergoes rigorous credential verification, background screening, and skills assessment. We do not compromise on clinical standards. Your patients' safety depends on it.
