What We Offer

Our Staffing Services

Accucare provides staffing across a range of care disciplines and settings throughout the Greater Houston area.

RN & LVN Skilled Nursing

Licensed RNs and LVNs for hospital and hospice assignments with verified credentials and current licensure.

CNA & Caregiver Support

CNAs and caregivers for long-term care and patient supervision needs. Compassionate, reliable, and background-verified.

Hospice Crisis Care Staffing

Continuous care nurses for hospice patients in medical crisis, available for urgent deployment when your team needs additional clinical support.

On-Call & After-Hours Coverage

Evening, overnight, weekend, and holiday staffing when you need it. Our team is reachable by phone around the clock.

Private Duty & Companion Care

One-on-one nurses, caregivers, and companion sitters for patients who need individualized attention, supervision, or companionship in home or facility settings.

Wound Care Support

Skilled nurses with wound care competencies for post-acute and home health patients requiring specialized dressing and assessment.
