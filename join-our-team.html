Career Opportunities

Join the Accucare Nurse Staffing Team in Houston

Accucare connects qualified nurses and caregivers with flexible per-diem opportunities across the Greater Houston area. Apply to join our network and work on a schedule that fits your availability.

Why Accucare

Why Work With Accucare?

Accucare is a Houston-based staffing agency with over two decades of experience placing nurses and caregivers in settings that match their skills and schedule.

Flexible Scheduling

Choose shifts that work around your life. Per-diem work means you set the pace: days, evenings, nights, or weekends. You decide when you're available.

Hospice & Specialty Placements

We specialize in hospice continuous care staffing, a good fit for RNs and LVNs with palliative or end-of-life care experience who want meaningful, purposeful work.

Local & Consistent

Work with facilities throughout the Greater Houston Area. As a Houston-based agency since 2004, we build long-term relationships with both our clinicians and our partner facilities.

Open Roles

Who We're Looking For

Registered Nurse (RN)

Active Texas RN license required. Hospice, home health, and acute care experience valued.

Licensed Vocational Nurse (LVN)

Active Texas LVN license required. LTC, hospice, and home health placements available.

Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA)

Texas CNA certification required. Long-term care, home health, and hospital sitter roles.

Caregiver / Sitter

Compassionate caregivers and patient sitters for private duty and facility assignments across Greater Houston.

Application Requirements

What You'll Need to Apply

Applying is quick and straightforward. Have the following ready before you start. That's all we need to get things moving.

Your resume summarizing your experience and work history
Basic contact info: name, email, and phone number
Your preferred start date and years of experience in your field
Answers to a few short eligibility questions (background check, work authorization, etc.)
