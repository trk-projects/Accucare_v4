What We Offer

Nurse Staffing Services in Houston, TX

Accucare provides credentialed nursing professionals across a range of care settings throughout the Greater Houston Area. Our team is available to assist with placement needs 24 hours a day.

Skilled Nursing

RN & LVN Skilled Nursing

Accucare provides licensed Registered Nurses and Licensed Vocational Nurses for hospital floors, hospice organizations, and care settings throughout the Greater Houston area. Each clinician is credentialed, background-checked, and matched to your specific assignment.

Whether you need a single shift covered or ongoing supplemental staffing, our nurses know their way around a range of care environments and can generally step in without a lengthy orientation.

Best for:  Hospitals, hospice agencies, private homes, and post-acute settings requiring licensed nursing coverage.

Paraprofessional Care

CNA & Caregiver Support

Certified Nursing Assistants and trained caregivers are the backbone of direct patient care. Accucare places credentialed CNAs and screened caregivers with long-term care facilities, home health agencies, and hospitals across the Houston area.

All CNA and caregiver placements are verified for certification status, background, TB clearance, and CPR credentials before they ever step into a facility or a patient's home.

Best for:  Families at home, skilled nursing facilities, assisted living, home health agencies, and hospitals requiring patient-assistance and daily living support.

Hospice Specialty

Hospice Crisis Care Staffing

When a hospice patient enters a period of acute medical crisis, continuous care nursing is required on short notice. Accucare maintains an on-call pool of RNs and LVNs with experience in palliative and end-of-life care settings, available for rapid deployment.

We understand the weight of these situations. Our clinicians bring care and attention to both the medical needs and the people involved, supporting patients and their families through a very hard time.

Best for:  Hospice organizations needing rapid deployment of continuous care nursing for patients in medical crisis, including overnight and weekend coverage.

One-on-One Care

Private Duty Care

Some patients require undivided, one-on-one attention that goes beyond what a care facility's standard staffing can provide. Accucare places private duty nurses and caregivers for patients requiring continuous, individualized care, whether in a home environment or a clinical facility.

Private duty assignments are matched carefully by specialty, experience, and patient needs. Our nurses and caregivers bring real skill and genuine care to the work, every time.

Best for:  Families at home, home health agencies, and facilities requiring one-on-one nursing or caregiver support for complex-care patients.

Safety Supervision

Patient Sitting Services

Patient sitters provide an essential safety function in hospitals, long-term care facilities, and behavioral health units, monitoring patients who are at risk of falls, elopement, or self-harm. Accucare places trained, attentive patient sitters who take this responsibility seriously.

Our patient sitters are trained in observation and communication with nursing staff, so patient safety stays solid without pulling nurses away from their other responsibilities.

Best for:  Hospitals, behavioral health units, skilled nursing facilities, and any care setting requiring one-to-one patient observation and safety supervision.

Clinical Specialty

Wound Care Support

Wound care is a specialized nursing skill needed across post-acute, home health, and long-term care settings. Accucare places skilled nurses with demonstrated wound care experience, including assessment, dressing changes, debridement preparation, and patient education.

Our wound care nursing staff have experience with complex wounds common in diabetic, post-surgical, and chronic care patients. Thorough documentation and care plan compliance are built into how they work.

Best for:  Patients at home, home health agencies, long-term care facilities, and post-acute settings requiring skilled wound care nursing on a per-visit or shift basis.
